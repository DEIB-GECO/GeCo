Gong-like presentations workshop

Workshop at Dipartimento di Elettronica, Informazione e Bioingeneria, Politecnico di Milano – February 27th 2020

Organized by the Advanced ERC Project 693174 “GeCo” – data-driven Genomic Computing

GeCo PhD students and master students, in collaboration with the Data Science group, communicated their research through short presentations (5 minutes, gong-like).
