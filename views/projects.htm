GENDATA

The GenData 2020 project (March 2013 - February 2016, PRIN, Funded by MIUR) has been conducted by nine universities throughout Italy.

During the project we designed the Genomic Data Model (GDM) and the GenoMetric Query Language (GMQL). In addition to independent research conducted by each Project Partner, several prototypes were jointly developed; among them:

  • PaGeS, a tool for finding Patterns in Genomic Sequences jointly developed with University of Bologna
  • SOSGeM, a semantic data integrator for supporting search upon ENCODE metadata, jointly developed with Sapienza University of Rome
  • TCGA2BED, for converting TCGA data to GDM, developed with University of Rome
